# Form Master User Manual
## AlifMonitor — School Early Warning Support System

**Version**: 1.0  
**Audience**: Form Masters  
**System URL**: https://www.alifmonitor.com

---

## What This System Does for You

AlifMonitor gives you a real-time view of your classroom's attendance health, automatically identifies students at risk, and provides a structured workflow to manage interventions. You receive alerts from teachers, open intervention cases, track progress, and escalate to administration when needed — all in one place.

You are responsible for **one classroom**. Everything you see in this system is scoped to that classroom only.

---

## 1. Logging In

1. Open your browser and go to **https://www.alifmonitor.com**
2. Enter your **email address** and **password**
3. Click **Login**

**Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):** If your administrator has enabled mandatory 2FA for your account, you will be asked for a 6-digit code from your authenticator app after entering your password. Open Google Authenticator or Microsoft Authenticator on your phone and enter the code shown.

**If login fails:**
- Check Caps Lock — passwords are case-sensitive
- After many consecutive failed attempts your account is temporarily locked for 15 minutes
- Contact your administrator if you have forgotten your password

---

## 2. Your Dashboard — Overview Tab

After logging in you land on the **Form Master Dashboard**. The Overview tab is your starting point every day.

### KPI Cards (top row)

Four summary numbers at the top:
- **Total Cases** — all intervention cases assigned to you
- **Open Cases** — cases not yet resolved
- **In Progress** — cases actively being worked on
- **Closed Cases** — resolved cases

### Urgent Banner

If any student requires immediate attention (high risk score, no intervention case open), a red banner appears at the top. Act on these first.

### Immediate Attention Widget

Lists up to 5 students who are high-risk but have no intervention case open yet. Click **Create Case** next to a student to open one immediately.

### High-Risk Students Table

All students in your classroom ranked by risk score. You can filter by risk level (Critical / High / Moderate / Low) using the filter buttons above the table. Click **Create Case** on any student to start an intervention.

### Classroom Stats

A summary card for your classroom showing attendance rate, absent count, and health status (Healthy / Moderate / Critical) based on the last 30 days.

### Charts

- **Case Status Distribution** — a pie chart showing how your cases are distributed across Open, In Progress, and Escalated statuses
- **Absence Trend Analysis** — a bar chart showing weekly absence counts for your classroom over the last 6 weeks

---

## 3. My Class

Click **My Class** in the sidebar to see your full classroom roster with each student's attendance history. Click any student's name to open their detail page, which shows:

- Their attendance record session by session
- Their risk score and risk level
- Cross-subject absence patterns (dates where they were absent in 2 or more subjects on the same day)

---

## 4. Alerts Tab

Click **Alerts** in the sidebar to see all risk alerts assigned to you. Alerts are created in two ways:
- **Automatically** — when a student has 3 or more consecutive absences, the system creates an alert and assigns it to you
- **Manually** — a teacher creates an alert for a student they are concerned about

### Reviewing an Alert

1. Find the alert in the list
2. Click **Review**
3. A dialog opens — enter your observation, set an urgency level, and optionally set a follow-up date
4. Click **Start Review** — this marks the alert as Under Review and automatically creates an intervention case

---

## 5. Cases Tab — Managing Interventions

Click **Cases** in the sidebar to see all intervention cases assigned to you.

### Case Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Open | Case created, no action taken yet |
| In Progress | You are actively working on this case |
| Escalated to Admin | You have escalated to administration for family contact |
| Closed | Case resolved |

### Updating a Case

1. Find the case in the table
2. Click the case to open it
3. Update the progress status, add outcome notes, or set a follow-up date
4. Save

Cases open for more than 14 days without an update are marked **overdue** in red.

### Escalating a Case to Admin

If a student's situation requires family contact or administrative action:

1. Open the case
2. Click **Escalate to Admin**
3. A confirmation dialog appears — confirm the escalation
4. The administrator receives an email notification automatically
5. This action cannot be undone

### Filtering Cases by Date

Use the date range filter at the top of the Cases tab to view cases created within a specific period.

---

## 6. High Risk Tab

Click **High Risk** in the sidebar to see the full list of at-risk students in your classroom. This is the same table as on the Overview tab but without the other dashboard sections — useful when you want to focus only on students.

---

## 7. Daily Monitor

Click **Daily Monitor** in the sidebar to see today's attendance for your classroom across all subjects and periods. This gives you a real-time view of who is in school today.

---

## 8. AI Insights

Click **AI Insights** in the sidebar to see:

- **Classroom Health Score** (0–100) — an overall score based on attendance and risk data
- **Risk Distribution** — how many students are at Critical, High, Moderate, and Low risk
- **Recommendations** for the top 5 highest-risk students — each recommendation includes a suggested action, the reason for it, and a priority level (Urgent / High / Moderate / Low)

These recommendations are generated from attendance patterns in your classroom data. They are suggestions to support your professional judgement, not instructions.

---

## 9. Bulk Analysis

Click **Bulk Analysis** in the sidebar to run a risk analysis across all students in your classroom at once. This is useful at the start of a term or after a holiday period to identify which students need attention.

---

## 10. Weekly Report

Click **Weekly Report** in the sidebar to view a summary of your classroom's attendance and intervention activity for the current week, including trends compared to the previous week.

---

## 11. Progress Tracking

Click **Progress Tracking** in the sidebar to monitor how students with open cases are progressing over time. This view shows whether attendance is improving, stable, or worsening for students under intervention.

---

## 12. Interventions Page

Click **Interventions** in the sidebar to access a dedicated interventions management page. This is an alternative to the Cases tab on the dashboard and provides additional management options.

---

## 13. Messages

Click **Messages** in the sidebar to communicate with teachers in your classroom:

1. Click **New Message**
2. Select the teacher
3. Type your message and send

Messages are private between you and the teacher. Administrators can view message logs for compliance purposes.

---

## 14. Profile and Settings

### Changing Your Password

1. Click **Settings** in the sidebar
2. Go to the Security section
3. Enter your current password, then your new password
4. Passwords must be at least 8 characters and include: one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, one number, and one special character (example: `Pass123!`)

### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

- To enable: Settings → Security → Enable 2FA → scan the QR code with your authenticator app
- Once enabled, you enter a 6-digit code from the app each time you log in
- If your administrator has made 2FA mandatory for your role, you cannot disable it

---

## 15. Logging Out

Click **Logout** at the bottom of the sidebar. Your session expires automatically after 1 hour of inactivity, with a 5-minute warning before it ends.

---

## 16. Common Problems

| Problem | What to do |
|---|---|
| Dashboard shows no students | Your classroom may have no enrolled students — contact your administrator |
| Alert not appearing | The teacher may not have submitted attendance yet today |
| Cannot escalate a case | Only Open or In Progress cases can be escalated |
| Charts show no data | There may be no attendance records yet for your classroom this term |
| 2FA code rejected | Codes expire every 30 seconds — enter the current code shown in your app |
| Forgot password | Contact your administrator to reset it |

---

## 17. Data Privacy

All student data in this system is confidential. You are only permitted to view data for students in your assigned classroom. You must not:
- Share your login credentials with anyone
- Export or share student data outside official school channels
- Discuss individual student cases with people who are not involved in that student's support

Your actions in the system are logged for audit purposes with a 7-year retention period as required by school policy.
